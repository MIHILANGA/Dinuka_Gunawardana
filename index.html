Thelme Costume

AUGUST 12, 2023

The Thelme costume is a vibrant attire in low-country dance, beautifully arranged and used in shanthikarma ceremonies for community health and agriculture. The "pahatharata beraya," also known as "Dik beraya" or "goshaka beraya," is a unique drum used in these dances, especially in Yak thovil ceremonies, made from animal skins in the Ruhunu area.
